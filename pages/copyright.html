Copyright Violation

At Bloop, we take copyright infringement seriously and are committed to maintaining the intellectual property rights of authors and content creators. Any instance of copyright violation on our platform is subject to strict action in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Reporting Copyright Violations

If you believe that your copyrighted work has been used on Bloop in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, please promptly notify us by sending a detailed notice to our designated copyright agent. The notice should include specific information, such as identification of the copyrighted work, a description of the infringing material, your contact information, and a statement confirming your good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner.

Our Response to Copyright Violations

Upon receiving a valid copyright infringement notice, we will investigate the reported material and take appropriate actions, which may include removing or disabling access to the infringing content. We will also notify the alleged infringer and provide them with an opportunity to respond. If the claim is substantiated, we will take prompt action to address the violation.

Counter Notification Process

If you believe that your content was mistakenly identified as infringing, you have the right to submit a counter-notification. This process involves providing specific information, including identification of the material in question, a statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed or disabled as a result of mistake or misidentification, and your consent to the jurisdiction of the federal court.

Legal Consequences

Know that submitting false claims or counter-notifications may lead to legal consequences. We encourage all users to respect intellectual property rights and adhere to copyright laws. If you have questions or concerns about copyright issues on Bloop, please contact our support team for assistance.
