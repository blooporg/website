Personal Harassment Policy:

At Bloop, we are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users. Personal harassment, in any form, is strictly prohibited, and we have implemented a robust policy to address such incidents promptly.

Definition of Personal Harassment:

Personal harassment includes but is not limited to unwelcome behavior, comments, or actions that create an intimidating, hostile, or offensive environment for an individual. This encompasses both online interactions within the Bloop platform and any associated communication channels.

Reporting Personal Harassment:

If you experience or witness personal harassment on Bloop, please report it immediately. Utilize our reporting mechanisms to bring the issue to our attention, and provide detailed information about the incident, including specific instances and involved parties.

Investigation and Action:

Upon receiving a harassment report, we will conduct a thorough investigation to assess the validity of the claims. Appropriate actions will be taken based on the severity of the incident, ranging from warnings and temporary suspensions to permanent account terminations, as deemed necessary.

Confidentiality and Support:

We prioritize the confidentiality of individuals involved in harassment cases. Our support team will handle the investigation discreetly, respecting the privacy of the affected parties. Additionally, we encourage victims to seek support from friends, family, or relevant authorities as needed.

Prevention and Community Education:

Bloop is committed to preventing personal harassment through community education. We regularly communicate our policies and expectations to users and provide resources to help foster a positive and respectful atmosphere for everyone.

Zero Tolerance:

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for personal harassment. Bloop is dedicated to creating a platform where users can engage without fear of harassment, ensuring a welcoming space for readers and authors alike.


Thank you for your cooperation in upholding a respectful community on Bloop. Together, we can contribute to a positive and inclusive environment for all users.
