Permissible Topics:

Islam encourages discussions and writings on various topics that align with its principles and contribute positively to society. Writers on Bloop can create content around the following permissible topics:

  • Islamic teachings and principles
  • Biographies of prophets and significant Islamic figures
  • Positive aspects of Islamic culture and history
  • Educational content on Islamic rituals and practices
  • Guidance on ethical business practices in Islam
  • Islamic finance and economic principles
  • Health and well-being from an Islamic perspective
  • Family and interpersonal relationships in Islam
  • Community service and charitable initiatives
  • Science and technology within the framework of Islamic ethics
  • Environmental awareness and sustainable practices in Islam
  • Islamic art, literature, and cultural expressions
  • Moral and character development based on Islamic teachings

Writers are encouraged to explore and contribute content that educates, inspires, and aligns with the values of Islam. Bloop aims to provide a platform for positive and constructive discussions that contribute to the enrichment of the Muslim community and beyond.

Diversity of Content on Bloop:

Bloop is not limited to Islamic literature alone; it embraces a diverse range of subjects and content that contribute to the holistic development and knowledge enrichment of its community. The platform encourages writers to explore and create content in various fields, including but not limited to:

  • Science
  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Computer Science
  • Self-help
  • Psychology
  • Neurology
  • much more

Writers on Bloop are empowered to share their expertise, insights, and knowledge across a broad spectrum of topics, promoting an inclusive and intellectually vibrant community. The platform recognizes the importance of embracing diversity and providing a space for discussions and learning beyond Islamic literature.


By covering subjects like science, math, physics, computer science, self-help, psychology, and more, Bloop aims to foster an environment where individuals can engage with a wide array of content that aligns with ethical and moral principles while contributing to personal and collective growth.
