Author Inclusivity on Bloop:

Bloop values and welcomes a diverse community of authors, embracing individuals from various religions, beliefs, and backgrounds. The platform is open to authors of all faiths, recognizing that knowledge and wisdom can be shared across cultural and religious boundaries.


Authors on Bloop are encouraged to contribute content that aligns with Islamic principles. While the platform welcomes individuals of different religious beliefs, it strictly prohibits the promotion or endorsement of beliefs contrary to Islam. Authors are allowed to provide educational content, including science, technology, and other subjects, as long as it adheres to the values and guidelines set by Bloop.


Whether you're a Muslim author sharing insights into Islamic teachings or an author from a different faith providing educational content that aligns with Islamic values, Bloop values the diversity of perspectives. However, the platform maintains a clear stance against the promotion of beliefs that contradict Islamic principles. Authors are encouraged to contribute positively to the platform's vision of fostering knowledge, understanding, and unity.


If you don't know which topics and books to promote then visit this page.
