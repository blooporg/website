About Me:


Hello! I'm Bilal, a Backend Engineer, Golang Developer, and an AWS Community Builder. This is my first startup, and I'm building it in public to provide an awesome service. I've contributed to Linux for Pirates, Canonical, Kubernetes, and Sourcegraph, currently focusing on Weaviate. As a Technical Writer at Hashnode, I share insights on Golang, databases, and DevOps. On YouTube (ibilalkayy), I create project-based videos with over 1000 subscribers and 28k+ views. Associated with the Data on Kubernetes Community, I actively contribute and share knowledge.


Previously, I closed 47 deals as a Customer Sales Representative at Prime BPO. Educationally, I graduated in computer science from Punjab Group of Colleges, Lahore. Currently, I'm developing Bloop, a subscription-based platform connecting authors and readers, offering an immersive reading experience. Passionate about teaching and community care, I'm driven to assist newcomers in their tech journeys.


Thank you for your support!
