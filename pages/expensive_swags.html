Why Swags are expensive on Bloop?

At Bloop, we believe in offering high-quality, customized swags to our community members. The pricing of our swags reflects the careful consideration we put into creating a product that not only represents Bloop but also meets the expectations of our users.

1. Customization and Quality:

Each swag item is carefully customized through Printful, featuring the distinctive Bloop logo. We prioritize quality to ensure that our community members receive products that are durable, comfortable, and showcase their association with Bloop in a meaningful way.

2. Transaction Costs:

When purchasing swags on Bloop, the transaction is facilitated through Buy Me a Coffee, a platform that supports content creators. While this provides a secure payment process, it incurs a 5% transaction fee. We want to be transparent about this cost, and despite it, we have kept the profit margin minimal to make these swags more accessible to you.

3. Affordable Pricing:

Despite the transaction fee and customization costs, we have worked diligently to keep the overall pricing of our swags as affordable as possible. Our intention is to provide our community with the opportunity to proudly wear and showcase their affiliation with Bloop without breaking the bank.

Your Support Matters:

By investing in Bloop swags, you are not only getting a stylish and personalized product but also supporting the platform and its endeavors. The modest profit from swag sales directly contributes to maintaining and enhancing the Bloop community and its offerings.

Thank You for Your Understanding:

We appreciate your understanding of the factors that contribute to the pricing of Bloop swags. Your support and enthusiasm for our community are invaluable, and we remain committed to delivering products that reflect the quality and spirit of Bloop.
