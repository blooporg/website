Community Code of Conduct:

We strive to maintain a positive and inclusive community on Bloop, fostering a space where readers and writers can engage respectfully. Our Community Code of Conduct outlines the expected behavior within the platform to ensure a harmonious environment for everyone.

1. Respect and Kindness:

The cornerstone of our community is respect. Treat others with kindness, empathy, and understanding. Engage in constructive discussions, even in times of disagreement, and refrain from offensive or harmful behavior.

2. Inclusive Content Guidelines:

We encourage creativity and diverse perspectives. However, to sustain a welcoming community, users are urged to avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content. Let's make Bloop a space where everyone feels comfortable and valued.

3. Building a Supportive Community:

Actively contribute to fostering a supportive environment. Share recommendations, resources, and engage in discussions that uplift and inspire fellow readers and authors. Lean on the community for assistance, recommendations, and shared enjoyment of books.

4. Termination and Fair Process:

In extreme cases of violation of our terms or community guidelines, Bloop reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts. We believe in a fair and transparent process, and affected users will be notified of any such actions.

5. Feedback and Constructive Communication:

We value open communication. If you have suggestions or concerns about the platform, please share them with our support team. Constructive feedback helps us continuously improve Bloop for the benefit of the entire community.

6. Mutual Respect Between Authors and Readers:

Authors and readers play vital roles in our community. Authors are encouraged to engage with readers respectfully, offering insights, answering questions, and fostering a positive connection. Readers, in turn, are urged to appreciate the effort and creativity of authors.

7. Enjoying Bloop Responsibly:

Use Bloop responsibly, respecting the rights and privacy of others. Uphold the values of our community code, and contribute to making Bloop a welcoming and enjoyable platform for all.


Thank you for being part of the Bloop community. Your adherence to this code of conduct helps create a vibrant and positive space for everyone to enjoy.
